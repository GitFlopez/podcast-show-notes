## What This Skill Does

Transforms your podcast episode transcript or bullet-point summary into a complete, SEO-optimized show notes package in under 60 seconds.

Podcasters spend 30-90 minutes writing show notes per episode. This skill does it in one prompt.

## Output Package (4 Prompts)

### 1. Full Show Notes (SEO-Optimized)
- Episode summary (150-200 words)
- Timestamped chapter markers
- Key takeaways (5-7 bullets)
- Guest bio paragraph (if applicable)
- Resources & links section
- SEO-friendly H2/H3 structure

### 2. Episode Description (for Apple/Spotify)
- Hook sentence (first 5 words matter most)
- 150-200 word description optimized for podcast directories
- Keyword-rich but natural-sounding

### 3. Social Clips Script
- 3 pull quotes formatted for Twitter/X
- 1 LinkedIn post (200-250 words with hook)
- 1 Instagram caption (100-130 words + 15 hashtags)

### 4. Email Newsletter Segment
- "This week on the podcast" segment (100-150 words)
- Plug-and-play into existing newsletter
- Includes CTA to listen

## Inputs Required

- Episode title
- Guest name and title (if applicable)
- Episode transcript OR detailed bullet points (what was discussed)
- Episode number
- Key resources mentioned

## Who This Is For

- Solo podcasters (1 person show, no team)
- Podcast production agencies
- Content managers handling multiple shows
- Coaches/consultants with branded podcasts
