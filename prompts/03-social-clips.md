# Prompt 3: Social Media Clips & Quotes

## When to Use
Use this after recording to generate the social content that drives episode traffic. One prompt → everything you need for a full week of posts around one episode.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a social media strategist specializing in podcast promotion. Your job is to extract the most shareable moments from a podcast episode and turn them into platform-native content.

Generate social media content for this podcast episode:

**Episode Details:**
- Podcast name: [YOUR PODCAST NAME]
- Episode number: [Ep. XX]
- Episode title: [EPISODE TITLE]
- Guest name: [NAME] (or "Host" if solo)
- Episode URL or placeholder: [URL]

**Episode Content:**
[PASTE TRANSCRIPT EXCERPT OR DETAILED NOTES — at minimum 200 words of content]

**Tone of your show:** [Choose: Professional / Casual / Inspirational / Educational / Entertaining]

---

Generate the following:

**3 Twitter/X Quote Cards**
Each quote:
- 1 powerful sentence pulled directly from the episode (in quotation marks)
- Attributed to [Guest Name] or [Host Name]
- 1-2 sentence setup before the quote (what context makes this land)
- 1 relevant hashtag + #[YourPodcastName]
- Character count must be under 280 including the quote and setup

**1 LinkedIn Post**
- Length: 200-250 words
- Hook line (first line, no intro fluff — makes them stop scrolling)
- 3-4 short paragraphs. Each paragraph = 1-2 sentences max.
- Key insight from the episode stated directly
- Soft CTA: "Full episode in comments" or "Link in bio"
- Professional but human tone. No corporate-speak.

**1 Instagram Caption**
- Length: 100-130 words
- Strong opening hook (first line visible before "more")
- 2-3 short paragraphs expanding the key insight
- CTA: "Link in bio to listen"
- 15 hashtags: mix of 3 broad (1M+ posts), 7 medium (50K-500K), 5 niche (under 50K)
- Hashtags on separate line at the end

Format each section clearly with headers.
```

---

## Scheduling Tips
- Twitter quotes: post Day 1, Day 3, Day 5 after episode drops
- LinkedIn post: post Day 1 or Day 2 (weekdays outperform weekends)
- Instagram: post Day 2-3 with an audiogram or episode cover as the image
