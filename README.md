# Podcast Show Notes Generator

**Stop spending 90 minutes writing show notes. Do it in 60 seconds.**

---

## The Problem

Every podcast episode needs:
- SEO-optimized show notes (so Google finds you)
- An Apple/Spotify description (so listeners click play)
- Social content (so your episode doesn't die in a feed)
- Newsletter segment (so your email list knows you shipped)

Most podcasters either skip half of this or pay $20-50 per episode for a VA to do it. Neither is good.

## The Solution

Four battle-tested AI prompts that generate a complete episode package from a transcript or bullet points. Paste your inputs, get publish-ready content.

## What You Get

| Prompt | Output | Time Saved |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Full Show Notes | SEO page with chapters, takeaways, links | ~60 min |
| Episode Description | 150-200 words for Apple/Spotify | ~20 min |
| Social Clips | 3 tweets, LinkedIn post, Instagram caption | ~45 min |
| Newsletter Segment | Plug-and-play newsletter section | ~15 min |

**Total time saved per episode: ~2.5 hours**
